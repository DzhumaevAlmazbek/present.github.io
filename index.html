Meeting in the sky


When I was in the darkness
Not knowing what to do,
I met you in the sky
Surrounded by clouds,
As white as the candle
You gave me that time.

You introduced me to your friends,
Who became my friends as well.
Endy, Dara, you and me,
We were getting closer day by day,
Spending time, collecting lights,
We did it all together.

It's already three months left
Since we met each other.
And I was happy all this time
Because you, baby,
Became my light, the light
That makes me feel alright.

Paths


I choose a path for me.
The path that goes to you.
It's full of pits and obstacles,
But I'm gonna break through.

If we meet one day,
I won't let you go.
Cause our paths will
Merge into one.

It may last long,
It may be short.
But in the end
We just need to be strong.

I wish you live happily
And smile as always.
This beautiful smile,
This beautiful and gorgeous.

Between Us


Everything what we have,
Everything what we feel,
It's the thing that inside us,
It's the thing that between.

My feelings and your feelings,
My warmth and your warmth,
Let them fill the space in our hearts,
Let them exist between us.

Thief of my heart


I have to find the cunning thief,
Who stole the heart of mine.
One day we have to face each other,
I hope until this she'll be fine.

She filled the space in my heart
And made me feel alive.
Sometimes I don't know what to say,
When she's about to cry.

She's feeling not that good these days,
So I wanna make her shine.
Shine as the eyes of the beautiful thief,
Who stole the heart of mine.

Let me know


I don't know what you feel,
When you're quiet and peaceful,
Keeping everything inside,
Feeling sad about it.

I want to know why you're upset,
Cause I know how it feels
When you have many things in your mind,
But have no one to reveal.

When you think that you're alone,
When you need someone to come,
When the sadness is being born,
Remember about everything you have.

About your family and relatives,
About your friends and collectives,
About your hometown and house,
About your baby who loves.

My Light


Every word you said to me,
Every moment we spent together.
It makes me think about you.
I think whole day about you.

And when you say that you're okay,
You don't act like usually.
And I can't fully trust you,
Cause I'm worrying about you.

I hate the fact that I can't help you,
And I blame myself for doing nothing.
But I want to find solution
To make you feel much better.

What I write belongs to you,
And my heart belongs to you.
Will you give me yours too?
Will you love me as I love you?
